Book a Junior Lesson

Burnaby's junior golf development programs give young golfers the opportunity to receive proper instruction on how to play the game, including rules and etiquette. Besides offering an excellent outlet for youthful energy, the game of golf teaches valuable communication skills and self-discipline while evoking the spirit of competition. The games rules, etiquette and sense of fair play reinforce life skills that young golfers can carry into everyday situations off the course.
