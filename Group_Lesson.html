Book a Group Lesson

Burnaby's golf development programs offer some of the finest and most comprehensive instruction available The goal of our friendly, knowledgeable CPGA professionals is to ensure that all students, from those learning basic fundamentals to those committed to long-term personal improvement, can maximize their own golfing potential. Golf development programs are tailored to the needs of each individual. At any age or level, we promise to make golf more enjoyable for you! Our adult classes are limited to maximum of six students to ensure a quality learning experience for everyone.

All group lessons include:

  • Complimentary use of equipment during lesson series (if required)
  • Pre-loaded driving range card (150 balls) for individual practice
  • Supervised practice sessions available between lessons
  • One complimentary pitch and putt round
  • Special discounts on equipment purchases
  • Custom equipment selection

Please Note:

  • If you have your own equipment, please bring it to your lessons.
  • Please arrive 15 minutes early.
  • With heated, dry stalls at both driving ranges, most lessons are unaffected by poor weather. If in doubt, contact the pro shop.
  • Taxes are included in prices.
  • 72 hours notice is required for program withdrawals

Register early - space is limited. To Register for lesssons, please call Burnaby Mountain (604-280-7355) or Riverway (604-280-4653) golf courses, after 1:00 p.m. if possible. For information about our refund policy
