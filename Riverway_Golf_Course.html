Riverway Golf Course

TThe Riverway Golf Course, located off of Marine Drive in South Burnaby and less than ten miles east of the Vancouver airport, is a classic 18-hole championship golf course with challenging and diverse yardage of 5,400 - 7,000. Its imaginately designed links-style fairways are lined by sweeping mounds of wild fescure. An array of white tan bunkers and water hazardss are strategically placed to make every shot exciting! Riverway is proud to be one of the premier championship golf courses in the greaterVancouver area and surrounding lower mainland. We sincerely hope your experience here will be both exciting and rewarding!

Take advantage of our full range of services! Our full-service Pro Shop offers brand name golf equipment and golf attire at competitive prices. We provide full-service rentals of golf clubs, pull carts and power carts and our CPGA Professionals are available for custom club fitting. Our driving range and practice facilities are perfect places to work on your skills. 54-stall covered and climate controlled driving range is night-lit for your convenience. The stunning new Riverway Clubhouse is ready to take care of all your tournament, pre or post game meals! In order to enhance play for all golfers, we appreciate your co-operation in observing our local rules listed in our Pro Shop while you are on the course.
