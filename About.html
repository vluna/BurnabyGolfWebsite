About Us

The City of Burnaby operates four high-quality golf facilities which are professionally maintained year-round for your recreational enjoyment. Burnaby's natural beauty and spectacular public golf facilities offer many of the luxuries of a private club right in the heart of the lower mainland. We pride ourselves in offering golf courses with unique designs and outstanding year-round playing conditions that offer the challenges and enjoyment for golfers of all ages and abilities to enjoy within a competitive, fair price structure. Enjoy the peaceful park-like settings of our two exceptional 18 hole golf courses - Riverway and Burnaby Mountain - and our two challenging pitch and putts (Kensington and Central Park). We also have two industry-leading Driving Ranges / Learning & Improvement Centers. Burnaby Golf has a long standing reputation for developing and teaching the game in local schools and driving interest for youth and new players through our wide variety of programs.

Call us and come see what makes Burnaby Golf so popular in the greater Vancouver and Lower Mainland areas! Please click on the facilities tab for more information about each site!
