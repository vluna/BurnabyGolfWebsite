Lessons & Programs

Group Lessons

Burnaby's golf development programs offer some of the finest and most comprehensive instruction available. The goal of our friendly, knowledgeable CPGA professionals is to ensure that all students, from those learning basic fundamentals to those committed to long-term personal improvement, can maximize their own golfing potential.

Junior Lessons

Burnaby's junior golf development programs give young golfers the opportunity to recieve proper instruction on how to play the game, including rules and etiquette. Besides offering an excellent outlet for youthful energy, the game of golf teaches valuable communication skills and self-discipline while evoking the spirit of competition.

Private/Semi Private Lessons

Private or semi-private lesson packages are available year-round. Tailored specifically for each golfer, this popular format allows for maximum flexibility in lesson times and content. All prices noted below are per person. Whenever possible, please provide a minimum of 72 hours notice for rescheduling.
