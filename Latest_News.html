Golf Tips

"The journey of your golf game. The challenge, keep it simple"

The process of learning to play the game of golf is a wonderful journey that many people expect to take less time than it does. The intrigue that surrounds the game is that no one ever completely masters it yet we love the challenge of trying to! The best golfers to ever play the game have each spent their lifetime honing their skills and overcoming their respective swing challenges. The theory of keeping the basics of rhythm, timing and balance in perspective never goes out of style.

Unfortunately today, with the advent of television’s golf channel and the never-ending list of golf books and videos, the confusion that ensues from conflicting information always challenges the “keep it simple” theory. The temptation of the “quick fix” is quite often too great for the average player to turn down. When you feel tempted, remember that not every tip applies to every golfer.

Be discerning when accepting free advice on your golf swing, especially from an unqualified source. A better bet is to spend some time with a Golf Professional – one who will help YOU with your individual style and abilities! And, it never hurts to PRACTICE YEAR ROUND! Burnaby Golf has assembled some of the most experienced instructional staff in the Lower Mainland at both Riverway and Burnaby Mountain Golf Courses.
