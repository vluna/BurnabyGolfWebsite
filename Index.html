Welcome to Burnaby Golf

Burnaby's natural beauty and excellent public golfing facilities give you many of the luxuries of a private club right in the heart of the lower mainland. Enjoy the peaceful and serene park like settings of our two exceptional 18 hole golf courses, two challenging Pitch & Putt layouts, and our industry leading Driving Ranges and golf improvement centres. Burnaby Golf has a long standing reputation for developing and teaching the game of golf in local schools and promoting interest for junior, beginner, and novice players through our wide variety of programs. We pride ourselves in offering golf courses with outstanding playing conditions year-round which offer challenges and enjoyment to golfers of all abilities at value driven rates.
